Our Story

From Service to Suds

Barry Boyd spent 29 years in the U.S. Coast Guard, retiring in 2014 after a career built on discipline, precision, and service. But his second calling had been brewing since 2003, when he poured his first homebrew.

After training under Greg Bentley at Black Cap Brewery in Pennsylvania, Barry and his wife Missy opened the doors of Barley Naked Brewing Company in October 2018. What started as a modest 7 barrel brewhouse quickly became Stafford's gathering place.

"Life is far too complicated and way too short, so relax, smile, and drink Barley Naked beer. It is simply perfection in each pint."

With Head Brewer Brady Fox (a JMU engineering grad) and Operations Manager Gary Preston keeping things running, the brewery has grown from a veteran's dream into an award-winning destination. But it still feels like walking into Barry and Missy's living room.

Our Process

Grain to Glass

Step 01

Source

Carefully selected grains, hops, and yeast. Minimal ingredients, maximum quality. Every recipe built on tradition.

Step 02

Brew

Handcrafted in our 7 barrel brewhouse. Each batch individually brewed by Head Brewer Brady Fox and the team.

Step 03

Condition

Patience. Each batch is properly conditioned until it reaches perfection. No shortcuts, no compromises.

Step 04

Pour

Fresh from our taps to your glass. The way beer should be enjoyed, in the company of good people.
